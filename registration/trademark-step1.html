Trademark Registration

Protect your brand name

Your CAC registration does not protect "Chiamaka's Kitchen". A trademark is the only legal way to own your brand name in Nigeria and stop others from using it.

This flow is coming soon. A Startora compliance specialist will reach out to guide you through the trademark registration process manually.
What you'll need
Your business name, logo (if applicable), description of goods/services, and class of trademark (there are 45 classes — Startora will help you identify yours).
Timeline & cost
₦60,000 total. Application to approval takes 6–9 months on the Nigerian Trademarks Registry. Startora handles all submissions and follow-up.
